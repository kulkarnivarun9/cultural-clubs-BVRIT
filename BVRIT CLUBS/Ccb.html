CULTURAL CLUB BVRIT

The Cultural Club of BVRIT (BVRIT Cultural Club) is a vibrant and dynamic student organization that celebrates the diverse cultural heritage of the college. With a passion for art, music, dance, and theater, the Cultural Club provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and engage in creative pursuits. Through a variety of events and activities, the club promotes cultural exchange, fosters unity, and encourages students to explore and appreciate different traditions and art forms. From organizing annual cultural festivals to hosting workshops and performances, the BVRIT Cultural Club creates an inclusive and energetic environment that enriches the college experience and helps students develop a deeper understanding and appreciation for the arts.

It was formed in 2011 but not named in 2016 it was officially named as CCB- CULTURAL CLUB BVRIT and was successfully running. It is well known for conducting events in the college. It has conducted events like

  • Traditional day inthe month of Jan every year
  • zealots in 2018
  • shubhakrutu - 2022
  • Athenes -2022
  • 25 years silver Jubilee celebrations -2022
  • kalos hours -2022
  • amogha -2022
