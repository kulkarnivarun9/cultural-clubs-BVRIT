CHALANA CHITRAM

Chalana Chitram Club is a vibrant and enthusiastic film club based in BVRIT Narsapur, a reputed engineering college. Comprising a group of passionate students who share a profound love for cinema, the club organizes regular screenings of diverse films, spanning various genres and languages. The club serves as a platform for film enthusiasts to come together, discuss, and appreciate the art of cinema. Through interactive sessions, workshops, and film festivals, Chalana Chitram Club fosters a deeper understanding of the cinematic world, while also promoting creative expression among its members.
